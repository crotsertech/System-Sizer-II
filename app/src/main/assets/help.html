What is System Sizer II?

System Sizer II is a free and open-source tool designed to estimate the correct water softener sizing based on real-world parameters:

  • Water Hardness
  • Iron
  • Manganese
  • Water Usage*

⚠️ Important Notice: In order to use this program, you MUST have a current water analysis that includes hardness, iron, and manganese levels.

*System Sizer II uses the US EPA 2025 estimate of 82 gallons/day/person to calculate usage.
