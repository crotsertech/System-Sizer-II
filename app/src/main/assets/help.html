How to Use This Program

  • Enter the water hardness (in grains per gallon, gpg) from your water analysis report.
  • Enter the iron and manganese levels (in ppm) exactly as shown in your report.
  • Input the number of people in your home to estimate daily water demand.
  • Select whether you want to use EcosoftÂ® ECOMIX C media (Yes/No).
  • Press the Calculate button to get your recommended system size.

Important Tips for Softener Sizing

  • Always base sizing on accurate and current water analysis data for best results.
  • Compensate for iron and manganese as they consume resin capacity and affect sizing.
  • Ensure the system is sized to handle your household's peak water demand.
  • Regular maintenance and timely regeneration are essential for system longevity.
  • Undersizing can cause premature resin exhaustion and poor water quality.
